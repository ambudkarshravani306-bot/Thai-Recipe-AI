Project Overview

What is Thai Recipe?

🎯 Purpose

A modern, fully interactive website that brings authentic Thai cooking to home chefs worldwide. Users can discover, browse, and learn recipes with step-by-step guides.

👥 Target Audience

  • Home cooks exploring Thai cuisine
  • Food enthusiasts seeking authentic flavours
  • Culinary students & professionals
  • Restaurant owners needing inspiration

🌍 Scope

120+ recipes across 18 categories. Available 24/7, powered by an AI assistant and backed by a secure user account system.

📌 Core Values

  • Authenticity — traditional Thai methods
  • Accessibility — clear, easy steps
  • Community — save & share recipes
  • Innovation — AI-driven assistance
